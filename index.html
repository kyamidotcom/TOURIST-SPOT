Must-Visit Tourist Spots

Explore the "Big Cave" with its stunning rock formations, underground pools, and challenging spelunking routes.

30 min from town 3-4 hours adventure

Bomod-ok Falls

Also known as the Big Falls, this majestic 200-foot waterfall is surrounded by rice terraces and lush vegetation.

About Sagada

Sagada is a small town in the Mountain Province of the Philippines known for its cool climate, stunning landscapes, and unique cultural heritage. Nestled in the Cordillera Mountains at about 1,500 meters above sea level, it offers a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation.

The town is famous for its hanging coffins, spectacular caves, majestic waterfalls, and the warm hospitality of its people, the Igorots. With temperatures ranging from 15-26Â°C, it's a perfect escape from the tropical heat.
